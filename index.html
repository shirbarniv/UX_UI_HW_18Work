Case Studies

Mobile App: Budget Buddy

Though most people love to travel, many will tell you that the most stressful part of traveling is budgeting their plans. To ease the travel experience, I designed a mobile budgeting app called Budget Buddy. By creating a platform for users to input spending budgets, add expenses and review financial statistics throughout their trip, I successfully helped users get the most out of their travel experience without breaking the bank or feeling overwhelmed.

Website Redesign: Rose Haven

As stay-at-home orders prevail around the world, there has been an upsurge in domestic violence and homelessness. In an attempt to help, my 4-person team and I reached out to Rose Haven, a non-profit aiming to help homeless women and non-binary people. We wanted to see if we can help them help others by creating a smoother user experience through their website. The new Rose Haven website was redesigned with visitors in mind, helping them achieve access to resources and services at greater ease. Simultaneously, we provided a quicker and more heartfelt path for donors desiring to contribute to their local community.
